Some Healthy Fruit drinks



CRANBERRY

Cranberry juice is high in potassium, antioxidants,vitamins C and E. It may also help prevent UTIs, though research on this effect is mixed.

BEET

They act as potent antioxidants, potentially lowering your risk of heart disease, inflammation, and certain types of cancer. Beet juice is also high in inorganic nitrates, which have been shown to increase athletic performance, decrease blood pressure and heart disease risk

APPLE

high in antioxidant compounds like flavonoids and chlorogenic acid, which help neutralize cell-damaging free radicals

ORANGE

Orange juice is a significant source of vitamin C, an antioxidant that is essential for skin health and iron absorption. It is also high in phenolic compounds, such as cinnamic, ferulic, and chlorogenic acids. These antioxidant compounds help fight free radicals, which can damage cells and lead to disease
